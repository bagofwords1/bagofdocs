Each chat session that is created will point to a report. A report will include all the generated tables, data models, visualizations, and more.

## Publish reports
---

Reports can be published to the web. This is a great way to share your reports with your team, or with the public. Click on the "Share" button in the report to set the report to public.

## Schedule reports
---

Reports can be scheduled to be rerun at a specific time
